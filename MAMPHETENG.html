RECIPE 1

YORKSHIRE PUDDING

INGREDIENTS
    3-oz flour
    1 egg
    1/4 teaspoon full salt 1/4 baking powder
    1 pont casilan-enriched milk
INSTRUCTIONS

  • *Serve the flour and salt into a bowl


  • *Pour the beaten egg into the centre of the flour and mix,adding the casilan-enriched milk gradually


  • *Beat from the centre until the mixture is of the consistency of thick cream and quite smooth


  • *Add the remainder of the milk and beat until the mixture contains air bubbles


  • *Leave to stand for at least 1/2 hour in a cool place


  • *Fold in baking-powder and pour into a dish containing a little hot fat


  • *Cook in a moderately hot oven for half and hour


  • *Oven temperature 380 degree F (139 Degree C)


Cook for 30 minutes

Protein content approximately 43 grams and serves 4 to 6 people
