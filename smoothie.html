STRAWBERRY-BANANA SMOOTHIE

RECIPE 3

    INGREDIENTS
  • 2 cups frozen strawberries

  • 1 ripe banana (room temperature)

  • 1 cup coconut milk ((light or full-fat) or sub almond, cashew or oat milk)

  • 1 tbsp agave syrup (or maple syrup to taste)

  • 1 tbsp nut butter (e. g. peanut butter, almond butter or cashew butter)

      • INSTRUCTIONS AND TIPS

    Step 1: Put your Cups Into the Refrigerator

  • Put the number of cups you are going to use to drink your smoothie in the refrigerator. This will help keep your smoothie cool while you are drinking it.

    • Step 2: Wash & Cut the Strawberries.

  • Wash the strawberries in a spaghetti strainer, thoroughly cleansing them with water. Then, spread the berries out on a plate and cut the stems of off each one. After that, cut each strawberry in half.

    • Step 3: Cut Bananas

  • Take 2 bananas and cut them both in half. Put them in another bowl for later use

    • Step 4: Ice Cream/Yogurt

  • Put 4 scoops of vanilla ice cream (vanilla yogurt can be used as a healthy alternative) into the blender.

    • Step 5: Fill the Blender

  • Pour the strawberries, bananas, ice cream/ yogurt, and one cup of ice (which will help keep the smoothie chilled) into the blender.

    • Step 6: Blend the Ingredients

  • Blend the ingredients on medium power for 1 minute, then on high for another minute.

    • Step 7: Pour & Enjoy

  • Pour the smoothie into the chilled cups, and enjoy the strawberry banana goodness!
