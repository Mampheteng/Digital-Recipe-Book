RECIPE 4

CHOCOLATE SMOOTHIE

INGREDIENTS


Bananas: 3
Ice: 2 cups
Organic Unsweetened Chocolate: Â½ cup
Brown cocoa Powder: 2 tbsp
Brewed Coffee: 8 fl. oz
Almond Milk: 2 cups
Cocoa Beans: Garnish

PREPERATIONS

Peel bananas and freeze.
Roast cocoa beans and prepare as directed on package.

DIRECTIONS

Add sliced frozen bananas, ice, organic unsweetened chocolate,
cocoa powder, brewed coffee and almond milk to blender and mix.
Garnish with roasted cocoa beans and serve.
